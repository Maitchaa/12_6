Italian Pasta Salad Recipe

Ingredients
180 gm premade pasta fusili 8 gm sliced black olives 2 cup sliced cherry tomatoes
oregano as required 1 1/4 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil 1 1/4 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil
3/4 teaspoon white vinegar 1/4 teaspoon sugar 1/2 cup chopped broccoli
1/2 cup grated low fat mozzarella cheese 2 teaspoon mixed herbs black pepper as required
salt as required 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

steps:

  1. Prepare the dressing: in a bowl, add olive oil, white vinegar, sugar, black pepper, salt, garlic powder, mixed herbs, and 2tbsp of water. Mix everything well and keep it aside.

  2. Mix veggies and pasta: Now, in another bowl, add broccoli, cherry tomatoes, and black olives. Then add cooked fusilli pasta (as per the instructions given in the package).

  3. Pour the dressing: Pour the dressing on the veggies and cooked pasta. Give it a nice toss so that everything mixes well.

  4. Your Italian Pasta Salad is ready to serve: Add mozzarella cheese and oregano on the top and it's done. Your appetizing Italian Pasta Salad is ready to serve.

Keto Lemon Chicken Recipe

Ingredients
200 gm chicken 1 cup hung curd salt as required 2 teaspoon spice paprika
1 handfuls coriander leaves 1 tablespoon garlic pastel 2 tablespoon lemon juice black pepper as required
1 tablespoon mixed herbs 1 tablespoon butter

steps:

  1. Wash chicken: To begin with this easy recipe, just wash and clean the chicken. Next, take a large bowl add lemon juice, curd, salt, pepper and 1 teaspoon paprika, whisk it well.

  2. Marinate the chicken: Prick the chicken pieces and coat the pieces with the mixture. Next, marinate the chicken and refrigerator for sometime.

  3. Cook the chicken: Take a pan and add 1 tablespoon butter along with ginger garlic paste. Then add the marinated chicken and keep stirring. Next, season the dish with remaining spices and herbs, cover the lid and allow it cook.

  4. Serve hot: Once the chicken is ready, reduce the flame and add in lemon slices and coriander. Serve hot and enjoy!

Potato and Corn Balls Recipe

Ingredients
1 large potato 1/2 cup parmesan cheese powder 1/2 teaspoon basil 4 teaspoon all purpose flour
1 cup corn 1/2 teaspoon black pepper 1/2 teaspoon garlic paste 1 cup vegetable oil

steps:

  1. To prepare Potato and Corn Balls, take one large potato and one cup corn and boil them in water separately. In a medium sized bowl, mash the boiled corn and potatoes. Grate the processed cheese and add it to the mashed corn and potatoes along with black pepper, garlic paste and salt. Mix well.

  2. Add dried basil, all purpose flour and dried oregano to the mixture above.

  3. Make small sized balls from the mashed mixture of corn and potatoes. Heat oil in a medium sized pan, and fry the corn balls on medium flame till they turn golden brown. Remove the corn balls from the pan and place them on tissue paper to soak excess oil.

  4. Serve hot with tomato ketchup.

Microwave Brownie Recipe

Ingredients
4 tablespoon chopped dark chocolate 6 tablespoon all purpose flour 6 tablespoon milk
2 tablespoon butter 4 teaspoon powdered sugar 1 Pinch salt

steps:

  1. Melt butter with chocolate: Add chopped dark chocolate to a bowl. Now add butter and microwave for 20 seconds. Mix the two well.

  2. Prepare the batter: In a bowl, take all-purpose flour, sugar, salt and mix them together. Add the chocolate mixture along with milk. Whisk well to make a smooth batter.

  3. Ready to be served: Once baked, chop into pieces, drizzle some chocolate sauce on it and serve the fudgy brownies.
