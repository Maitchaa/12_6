Italian Pasta Salad Recipe

Ingredients
Ingredient Quantity Notes

Sliced black olives 8 gm -
Cherry tomatoes (sliced) 2 cups -
Oregano As required -

Steps:

  1. Prepare the dressing by mixing olive oil, vinegar, sugar, and spices.
  2. Combine cooked pasta, vegetables, and dressing.
  3. Toss well and garnish with cheese and oregano.

Keto Lemon Chicken Recipe

Ingredients
Ingredient Quantity Notes
Chicken 200 gm -
Hung curd 1 cup -
Garlic paste 1 tbsp -
Lemon juice 2 tbsp -

Steps:

  1. Marinate chicken in curd, spices, and lemon juice.
  2. Cook in a butter-greased pan with garlic.
  3. Simmer and serve hot with garnish.
