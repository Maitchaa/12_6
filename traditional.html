Italian Pasta Salad Recipe

Ingredients
180 gm premade pasta fusilli 8 gm sliced black olives 2 cup sliced cherry tomatoes
oregano as required 1 1/4 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil 1 1/4 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil
3/4 teaspoon white vinegar 1/4 teaspoon sugar 1/2 cup chopped broccoli
1/2 cup grated low-fat mozzarella cheese 2 teaspoon mixed herbs black pepper as required
salt as required 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

Steps:

  1. Prepare the dressing: In a bowl, add olive oil, white vinegar, sugar, black pepper, salt, garlic powder, mixed herbs, and 2 tbsp of water. Mix well and keep aside.
  2. Mix veggies and pasta: In another bowl, add broccoli, cherry tomatoes, and black olives. Then add cooked fusilli pasta.
  3. Pour the dressing: Pour the dressing on the veggies and pasta. Toss well to mix everything.
  4. Top with cheese and oregano: Add mozzarella cheese and oregano on top. Ready to serve.

Keto Lemon Chicken Recipe

Ingredients
200 gm chicken 1 cup hung curd salt as required 2 teaspoon paprika spice
1 handful coriander leaves 1 tablespoon garlic paste 2 tablespoon lemon juice black pepper as required
1 tablespoon mixed herbs 1 tablespoon butter

Steps:

  1. Wash chicken: Clean the chicken and in a bowl, mix lemon juice, curd, salt, pepper, and 1 teaspoon paprika.
  2. Marinate the chicken: Prick the chicken pieces and coat with the mixture. Marinate and refrigerate for some time.
  3. Cook the chicken: In a pan, add butter and garlic paste. Add the marinated chicken, stir, season, and cook with lid on.
  4. Serve hot: Reduce heat, add lemon slices and coriander. Serve hot.

Potato and Corn Balls Recipe

Ingredients
1 large potato 1/2 cup parmesan cheese powder 1/2 teaspoon basil 4 teaspoon all-purpose flour
1 cup corn 1/2 teaspoon black pepper 1/2 teaspoon garlic paste 1 cup vegetable oil

Steps:

  1. Boil potato and corn: Mash them in a bowl, add cheese, black pepper, garlic paste, and salt.
  2. Mix ingredients: Add basil, flour, and oregano.
  3. Shape into balls: Fry the balls until golden brown. Drain excess oil.
  4. Serve with ketchup.

Microwave Brownie Recipe

Ingredients
4 tablespoon chopped dark chocolate 6 tablespoon all-purpose flour 6 tablespoon milk
2 tablespoon butter 4 teaspoon powdered sugar 1 pinch salt

Steps:

  1. Melt chocolate and butter in a microwave-safe bowl for 20 seconds. Mix well.
  2. Prepare batter: Mix flour, sugar, salt, add chocolate mixture and milk. Whisk.
  3. Serve: Bake, cut into pieces, and drizzle with chocolate sauce.
