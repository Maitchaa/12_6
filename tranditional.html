Italian Pasta Salad Recipe

Ingredients of Italian Pasta Salad
180 gm premade pasta fusili 8 gm sliced black olives 2 cup sliced cherry tomatoes
oregano as required 1 1/4 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil 1 1/4 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil
3/4 teaspoon white vinegar 1/4 teaspoon sugar 1/2 cup chopped broccoli
1/2 cup grated low fat mozzarella cheese 2 teaspoon mixed herbs black pepper as required
salt as required 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

steps:

  1. Step 1 Prepare the dressing: in a bowl, add olive oil, white vinegar, sugar, black pepper, salt, garlic powder, mixed herbs, and 2tbsp of water. Mix everything well and keep it aside.

  2. Step 2 Mix veggies and pasta: Now, in another bowl, add broccoli, cherry tomatoes, and black olives. Then add cooked fusilli pasta (as per the instructions given in the package).

  3. Step 3 Pour the dressing: Pour the dressing on the veggies and cooked pasta. Give it a nice toss so that everything mixes well.

  4. Step 4 Your Italian Pasta Salad is ready to serve: Add mozzarella cheese and oregano on the top and it's done. Your appetizing Italian Pasta Salad is ready to serve.
